CCSF Fall 2014 Etching Workshop

When / Where

Tuesdays 6:00 to 10:00 PM
September 16th to November 11th
Fort Mason, Bldg B Rm: 208

Materials

Required

  • Etching Needle
  • Round #2 Brush
  • Round #4 Brush
  • 1 Yard Tarletan wipe
  • Paper (Rives BFK, German Etch, Stonehenge, etc) *
  • Etching Plates (zinc for etching) *
  • Large Pad of Newsprint

Recommended

  • Scraper
  • Burnisher
  • Jewelers Loupe
  • Sharpie
  • Portfolio
  • Gloves *
* materials available to purchase in class
